Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project usually take?

It depends on the scope, but typically: Brand identity (1-2 weeks), Website (2-3 weeks), Complete package (3-4 weeks). I'll give you a specific timeline during our consultation.

What if I don't have a big budget?

I offer flexible packages designed for small businesses. We can also start with what's most urgent and build from there. Let's talk about what works for your budget!

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions. I want you to be 100% happy with the result. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

What do I need to provide?

Basic information about your business, target audience, and preferences. I'll guide you through a simple questionnaire. For websites, you'll need to provide content (or I can help write it).

How do payments work?

Typically 50% downpayment to start, 50% upon completion. I accept GCash, bank transfer, or PayPal. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.
